Utah County Accused of Falsifying Road Data For Money

How honest are Utah counties about the roads they claim are RS 2477 rights-of-way? One clue may be how honest the counties are about claiming what routes they maintain to get federal funds. And former county road managers don't think Uintah County in northeast Utah has been so honest about that. See the March 8, 2005, Salt Lake Tribune story. Read the October 14, 2003, complaint filed in federal court (unsealed in February 2005).
